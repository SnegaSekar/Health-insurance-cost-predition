Dataset Information

This dataset contains information on medical insurance costs based on various attributes like age, gender, BMI, number of children, smoking status, and region.

Attribute Description
Age The age of the individual.
Gender The gender of the individual (male/female).
BMI Body Mass Index of the individual.
Children The number of children/dependents covered by the insurance.
Smoker Smoking status of the individual (yes/no).
Alcoholic To know whether the individual is Alcoholic or not (yes/no).
Diabetes Diabetes status of the individual (yes/no).
Region The region where the individual resides (northwest, southeast, southwest, northeast).
Charges The medical insurance cost for the individual.
